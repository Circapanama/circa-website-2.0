Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Can foreigners buy property in Panama?

Yes, foreigners have the same property ownership rights as Panamanian citizens. There are no restrictions on foreign property ownership in Panama, making it one of the most accessible markets in Latin America for international buyers.

What is the Friendly Nations Visa?

The Friendly Nations Visa is a fast track residency program for citizens of 50+ countries including the US, Canada, UK, and most EU nations. It allows you to obtain permanent residency in Panama through economic or professional ties to the country.

How does Panama's tax system benefit property investors?

Panama uses a territorial tax system, meaning foreign sourced income is not taxed. There is no capital gains tax on primary residence sales, and the Pensionado program offers retirees significant discounts on healthcare, dining, travel, and entertainment.

What areas in Panama are best for real estate investment?

Top areas include Playa Venao for beachfront lifestyle and surf culture, Coco del Mar for modern urban waterfront living, Casco Viejo for historic charm and boutique appeal, and Boquete for mountain retreats and expat communities. Each offers unique investment profiles with rental yields averaging 7%+ annually.

What services does Circa Panama provide beyond property sales?

Circa Panama is a full service real estate partner covering buying, selling, investment advisory, legal support (title searches, contracts, residency), and complete relocation services (visa processing, banking setup, school enrollment, healthcare, and community integration).
